The Bioscience Problem

In food security and plantation management, harvesting timing is everything. This tool models the growth of a Malaysian DxP hybrid Fresh Fruit Bunch (FFB) over its 20-week development cycle.

While the raw weight curve shows total mass, the derivative f'(x) reveals the exact rate of production growth. Tracking this allows estate managers to accurately forecast peak nutrient demand and logistical workforce distribution.

1. Raw Bunch Mass Function f(x)

f(x) = L / (1 + e^(-k * (x - x0)))

Models total bunch weight (kg) at week x.

2. Growth Rate Derivative f'(x)

f'(x) = (L * k * e^(-k * (x - x0))) / (1 + e^(-k * (x - x0)))^2

Calculates instant growth velocity (kg/week) at week x.
